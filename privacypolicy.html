Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chore Champ! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Chore Champ:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your first name, last name, and date of birth.
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how you use the app, including features used, time spent on each screen, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the app, such as device model and operating system version.
  • Performance Data: We collect information about the app's performance, including crash reports and load times.

    • 3. How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Understand how users interact with the app.
  • Identify and fix bugs and performance issues.
  • Prioritize and enhance features based on usage patterns.

    • 4. Data Sharing

    We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or to protect our rights.

    5. Subscriptions

    Chore Champ offers subscriptions for additional features. We use Apple's subscription services to manage payments. Your payment information is handled by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

    6. Data Security

    We take your privacy seriously and use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information.

    7. Children's Privacy

    Chore Champ does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 8. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal information from a child under the age of 8, we will delete such information from our records.

    8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
